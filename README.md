### Project Overview -

I created this Pizza Sales Dashboard in Power BI to analyze sales data and understand customer ordering patterns. I analysed the data  and built dashboards  while extracting meaningful business insights from a real-world dataset.

The dashboard helps answer questions such as:

* How much revenue was generated?
* Which pizzas are selling the most and the least?
* Which pizza category contributes the most to sales?
* During which days and months do customers place the most orders?
* What pizza sizes are preferred by customers?

## Dashboard Overview

The report consists of two pages:

### 1. Home Dashboard

This page provides an overall view of the business performance.

It includes:

* Total Revenue
* Average Order Value
* Total Pizzas Sold
* Total Orders
* Average Pizzas per Order

It also shows:

* Daily order trends
* Monthly order trends
* Sales contribution by pizza category
* Sales contribution by pizza size
* Total pizzas sold by each category

From the analysis, I found that orders are generally higher on Fridays and Saturdays, especially during the evening. I also observed that January and July recorded the highest number of orders.

### 2. Best & Worst Sellers Dashboard

This page focuses on product performance.

It highlights:

* Top 5 pizzas by revenue
* Top 5 pizzas by quantity sold
* Top 5 pizzas by total orders

It also identifies:

* Bottom 5 pizzas by revenue
* Bottom 5 pizzas by quantity sold
* Bottom 5 pizzas by total orders

Through this analysis, I found that the Thai Chicken Pizza generated the highest revenue, while the Classic Deluxe Pizza received the highest number of orders and quantity sold. On the other hand, the Brie Carre Pizza performed the weakest across multiple metrics.
